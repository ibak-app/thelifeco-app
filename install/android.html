Install on Android

Add the app to your home screen in 30 seconds

i

Works with most browsers. Chrome, Samsung Internet, and Microsoft Edge all support PWA install on Android.

1
Open Chrome
Open the Chrome browser on your Android phone. You can also use Samsung Internet or Edge.
Chrome
2
Go to your guest portal
Type the URL in the address bar: thelifeco.app/guest/your-name/
thelifeco.app/guest/...
3
Tap the three-dot menu
Find the ⋮ (three vertical dots) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
thelifeco.app/guest/...
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
In the menu, look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" (wording varies by browser).
New tab
Add bookmark
Add to Home screen
Settings
5
Tap "Install" to confirm
A dialog will appear showing the app name and icon. Tap "Install" to add it.

You're all set!
The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it anytime to open your guest portal.
