Install on iPhone

Add the app to your home screen in 30 seconds

!

Safari required. PWA install only works in Safari on iOS. Chrome and Firefox on iPhone do not support this feature.

1
Open Safari
Find the Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad. It has a blue compass icon.
Safari
2
Go to your guest portal
Type the URL in the address bar: thelifeco.app/guest/your-name/
thelifeco.app/guest/...
3
Tap the Share button
Look for the square with an upward arrow at the bottom of Safari's toolbar.
thelifeco.app/guest/...
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet until you see the option. It has a plus (+) icon.
thelifeco.app
Copy
Add to Home Screen
Add Bookmark
5
Tap "Add" to confirm
You'll see a preview. Tap the blue "Add" button in the top-right corner.
Cancel
Add to Home
Add
6
You're all set!
The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it anytime to open your guest portal.
