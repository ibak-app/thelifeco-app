The Solution

One Link. Everything They Need.

Each guest receives a personalized PWA portal — accessible from any device, works offline, installable to home screen.

Smart Schedule

Timeline view of today's activities. Auto-highlights the current and next item in real time. Color-coded by appointment type. Tap any item to learn more.

Full Programme

Swipeable multi-day view spanning the entire stay. See every appointment, treatment, and group activity at a glance. Never miss an appointment again.

Therapy Explorer

Browse all available therapies by category. Rich descriptions and photos. One-tap WhatsApp booking to the therapy planner. Contextual recommendations by programme.

Everything Else

Facility guide, weekly activities, cancellation policies, FAQ, nutrition info, supplement guide — all in one place. No more hunting through PDFs at midnight.

Revenue Impact

Turn Information Into Revenue

A guest who can browse therapies at 10pm in their room is far more likely to book one than a guest who sat through a 20-minute group talk.

"Popular with [Programme] guests" badges

Social proof labels on therapy cards drive curiosity. Guests on the Master Cleanse see which add-ons others in their programme have chosen.

Contextual suggestions after clinical appointments

After a colonic irrigation, the portal can suggest supporting therapies like lymphatic drainage or a probiotic IV — already pre-filtered and explained.

One-tap "Request This" WhatsApp booking

A pre-filled WhatsApp message opens with the therapy name and guest details already typed. No friction. The guest just taps send — the therapy planner receives a clean request.

24/7 browsing replaces one time-pressured talk

Instead of a single high-pressure group session on day one, guests browse at their own pace throughout their stay — at breakfast, before bed, whenever it feels right.

Deployment

Zero Infrastructure. Instant Deployment.

No app store submissions. No backend servers. No ongoing maintenance costs. Just a link.

1

Generate

Staff enters guest data into a simple form or spreadsheet. A personalized HTML page is generated automatically — complete with schedule, programme details, and name.

2

Share

Guest receives their unique link via WhatsApp on check-in day. Example: thelifeco.app/guest/firstname-lastname — no login, no password, just tap and go.

3

Install

Guest taps "Add to Home Screen." The portal installs as a native-feeling app. Works offline after first visit. Updates automatically when staff modify the schedule.

Technical Architecture

Single .html file per guest. No app store approval. No backend. No database. No user accounts. No GDPR complexity. Hosted on GitHub Pages or Cloudflare Pages — total infrastructure cost: ~$0/month. Guest data lives only in the HTML file itself. Files can be password-protected or made private at any time.
